Snake, Gun, and Water Game

Choose your weapon:

How to Play:

1. Choose one of the options: Snake, Gun, or Water.

2. The computer will randomly select one as well.

3. The outcome will be displayed based on the rules:

  • Snake drinks Water (Snake wins)
  • Water destroys Gun (Water wins)
  • Gun shoots Snake (Gun wins)
  • If both you and the computer choose the same, it's a Tie.

NOTE:If you want the Computer to choose something else you need to reload the page.
